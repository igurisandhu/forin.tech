NO REFUND POLICY

Forin Tech operates on a NO REFUND policy. All sales are final and non-refundable. Once a purchase has been made for any of our products or services, payment cannot be reversed or returned.

1. Policy Details

This no refund policy applies to all products and services offered by Forin Tech, including but not limited to:

  • LinkPre subscriptions and upgrades
  • QueryBoat.ai subscriptions and services
  • Custom software development projects
  • Web development services
  • Mobile app development
  • AI and machine learning solutions
  • SaaS platforms and tools
  • Tech consultancy services
  • Any other Forin Tech products or services

2. Terms and Conditions

  • • All payments for Forin Tech products and services are final and cannot be refunded under any circumstances.
  • • No refunds will be issued for subscription cancellations, downgrades, or for unused portions of a subscription period.
  • • Customers are encouraged to thoroughly review service details before making a purchase.
  • • For custom development projects, a non-refundable deposit may be required, with the remainder due upon completion.
  • • We stand behind our products and services with ongoing support. If you experience issues, contact our support team for assistance.

3. Exceptions

Under certain exceptional circumstances, such as billing errors or fraudulent transactions, Forin Tech may, at its sole discretion, consider alternative remedies such as account credits or service extensions rather than monetary refunds.

4. Billing Disputes

If you believe you have been charged in error or have a billing dispute, please contact us immediately. We will investigate the matter and work to resolve any legitimate billing concerns. However, this does not constitute a refund but rather a correction of billing errors.

5. Subscription Management

Customers can cancel their subscriptions at any time. However, cancellation does not result in a refund for the current billing period or any unused service time. Cancellations will take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

6. Custom Project Payments

For custom development and consulting projects:

  • All project agreements will specify payment terms, milestones, and deliverables
  • Payment schedules are agreed upon before project commencement
  • Deposits are non-refundable once project work begins
  • Cancellation of projects may result in payment for work completed to date

7. Service Support

Instead of refunds, we offer:

Forin Tech reserves the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page, and continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
